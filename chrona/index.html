Sleep smarter, not longer

Chrona turns your pillow into a Smartpillow, bringing greater comfort and the benefits of emerging sleep research to your bed. Chrona actively enhances the quality of your sleep with sounds specific to where you are in your sleep cycle. Chrona is designed to compliment your high-performance lifestyle: Sleep better, without spending more time in bed.

Features

Instead of being attached to your wrist like other wearable trackers, Chrona is placed in your pillowcase, where it measures the movements of your head and torso. With the Chrona app, you’ll be able to see how well you slept and set Chrona's haptic alarm to wake you up without disturbing your partner.

Sound Sleep

Chrona’s uses research-tested sounds to actively improve the quality of sleep.

Deep Sleep Boost uses low-frequency sounds to help you sleep more deeply. Sounds in the delta range can synchronize and boost deep sleep, which ongoing studies show to have benefits for memory and cognition.

Perfect Wake-Up uses higher frequencies to help you enter a lighter state of sleep. When you’re relaxed with your eyes closed (ex. during meditation), your brain expresses the highest activity in the alpha range. Perfect Wake-Up uses sound in the same frequency range to prepare your mind to wake up.

Upgrade Your Pillow:
1 Step Setup

With Chrona, there’s no need to change your bedtime routine. Simply insert Chrona into your pillowcase and sleep as usual. Set an alarm using the Chrona app for a refreshing morning with Perfect Wake-Up and Chrona’s gentle vibrating alarm.

The Chrona System

Chrona uses an accelerometer to measure your movements while you sleep. Its Bluetooth Low Energy module safely transmits data between your Smartpillow and smartphone. Chrona is energy-efficient, so you'll be able to power Chrona for over a week with a single charge.

Our design process is focused around comfort and ease-of-use. Chrona’s thin latex foam insert adds a layer of comfort and cooling, without compromising the integrity of your pillow.
