About

  • 2013

    Ultradians

    Co-Founders Zimin Hang and Ben Bronsther team up with a talented group of Washington University in St. Louis engineering students to form Ultradia.

  • Early 2014

    A Design Pioneered

    Our ambitions culminate in an endeavor to transform sleep monitoring with a free-form EEG sensor sheet. We patent the design with the goal of providing more accurate and reliable at-home polysomnography.

  • Mid 2014

    A Prototype Completed

    After research into methods for enhancing sleep, we make a crucial pivot to create a system for active sleep optimization. We host our first demo of Chrona to a live audience at a launch party.

  • Late 2014

    Collaborators

    We search and connect with an amazing collection of industry and research leaders, whose expertise in the fields of connected devices, neuroimaging, and market strategy help us discover who we are.

  • End 2014

    Optimization

    We redefine Chrona to help it reach the people who need it the most. We begin our collaboration in the sleep lab to maximize Chrona's effectiveness.

  • 2015 & Beyond

    Ultradian Lifestyle

    Ultradia strives to become the pioneer in sleep optimization. We want to be the catalyst for people to sleep better and do more, without having to spend more time in bed.

  • Through the creation of an idiosyncratic sensory and feedback system, Ultradia will enhance and optimize the cardinal state of unconsciousness.
